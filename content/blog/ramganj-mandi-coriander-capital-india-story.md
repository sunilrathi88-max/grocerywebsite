# Ramganj Mandi: Why India's Coriander Capital Grows the World's Best Dhaniya

Ramganj Mandi in Kota district, Rajasthan is Asia's largest coriander trading market — and the reason Rathi Naturals has sourced its dhaniya from here for three generations. The region's semi-arid microclimate produces seeds with exceptionally high **Linalool** content.

## The Terroir of Kota Coriander

Just as champagne comes from a specific region in France, the best coriander in India comes from the Ramganj Mandi-Hadoti belt.

What makes it special?

- **The Soil**: High in potash, which helps the seed develop a thick, oil-rich hull.
- **The Climate**: Cold winters followed by a dry, hot spring. This stress' triggers the coriander plant to produce more defense compounds—which we know as aromatic essential oils.
- **The Variety**: The 'Badami' and 'Eagle' varieties grown here are world-famous for their citrusy, almost floral aroma.

## From Mandi to Sangaria: The Cold-Ground Path

Since 1965, the Rathi family has maintained direct relationships with farmers in this belt. We don't buy commodity-grade coriander from bulk wholesalers. We select the highest-oil-yield seeds and transport them to our facility in Sangaria for cold-grinding.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **The Science of Sourcing**
> Our coriander is sourced from the heart of the Hadoti belt, where the soil and climate create the world's most aromatic seeds. By cold-grinding these seeds below 10°C, we ensure that the delicate "Linalool" compounds—the source of that signature citrus scent—are preserved entirely. It's the difference between a flat, dusty powder and a vibrant, professional-grade spice.

## The 'Seed to Powder' Standards

Most coriander powder in India is 'Exhausted'. This means the seeds have already had their valuable oils removed via steam distillation for the perfumery industry before being ground into powder for food.

**Rathi Naturals Promise**:

1. **Never Exhausted**: Our seeds contain 100% of their natural oils.
2. **Cold-Ground**: We grind at sub-10°C to ensure those oils stay in the powder, not in the factory air.
3. **Pure Origin**: 100% Ramganj Mandi seeds—no blending with cheaper, flat-tasting varieties from other regions.

> [!NOTE]
> **Quality Check**
> Good coriander powder is all about the volatile oil content. These oils are sensitive to both heat and air. Cold-grinding provides a much higher "starting point" for freshness, and when stored in a cool, dark place, our dhaniya powder retains its potency and citrusy bite far longer than mass-market brands.
