## Step 3: Keyword Optimization for Syndication

Each syndication platform has different SEO weight. Medium articles rank well for "Indian spices" queries. LinkedIn articles reach industry professionals. Substack reaches home cooks.

**Strategy:** Publish same article with region-specific angles:

- Medium: "The Science of Regional Indian Spice Blends"
- LinkedIn: "Quality Standards for Premium Spice Brands (2025 FSSAI Compliance)"
- Substack: "Building Your Home Spice Pantry: A Beginner's Guide"

## Step 4: Canonical Link Setup

When syndicting, always include:

```html
<link rel="canonical" href="https://tattvaco.in/blog/article-name" />
```

This tells Google the original source is your site, passing SEO authority back home.
